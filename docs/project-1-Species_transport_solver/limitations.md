# Limitations

## Model Simplifications
- Single-species transport model
- No interaction between multiple species
- Reaction modeled as simple first-order decay

---

## Flow Assumptions
- Constant velocity field
- No coupling with Navier–Stokes equations
- No turbulence effects

---

## Numerical Limitations
- Explicit time stepping requires small time steps
- Grid resolution impacts accuracy
- No adaptive mesh refinement used in this setup

---

## Physical Simplifications
- No temperature coupling (no energy equation)
- Constant diffusion coefficient
- No real chemical kinetics

---

## Summary
This project focuses on understanding the fundamentals of scalar transport. While simplified, it provides a strong foundation for more complex CFD simulations.
